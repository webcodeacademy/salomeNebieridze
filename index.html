Introduction to Neuroscape

"Neuroscape" a Vision of Brilliant Minds. A first of its kind conference focused on Neurosurgery and Neurology pioneered by students of NVU. With a rich variety of ideas, experiences and perspectives, we are excited to share this project with our colleagues and the medical field at large; our dream is to builda better understanding of the field of Neurosurgery and Neurology. This is an opportunity to rub minds, be a part of this think tank and let's grow together. We aim to broaden the interests of medical students and health care workers towards the increasing plethora of research and new information rising in this area of medicine andHealthcare.
